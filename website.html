Principal's Desk

Sh. S.K. MISHRA, PRINCIPAL, JNV- BALLIA

The 1993 was the most auspicious day when the foundation stone of Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya, Sinhachaur, Ballia was laid down by Hon Shri Chandrashekhar, Ex- Prime Minister. The school campus covers around 27.5 Acres. These buildings include the beautiful old Quad, the modern state of art infrastructure and the newly-refurbished student hostels and staff houses. The academic buildings including several classrooms are linked by a computer network. The widespread use of email and an intranet system shrinks the distances and keeps staff, students and administration in quick and easy contact with one another. The Vidyalaya is surrounded by the grove of trees on the main Patna- Gazipur National Highway (NH-19), at Sinhachaur. The school is located 20 K.M before Ballia city
In the progress of the institution the institutional plan has a pivotal role. It is a programme of development and improvement .It is also prepared to look into the problems, difficulties and obstacles experienced through-out the year and the institution tries to find out the solutions within the resources available. For all round development of a child, active participation of everyone at each level is envisaged .Much scope has been provided to incorporate maximum activities to supplement and complement academics. Adequate provisions have been given to fulfill the aims of Navodaya Vidyalaya. In order to acquire the kudos and to achieve the benchmark desired and highlighted in the Vidyalaya’s Institutional planning all are requested to co-operate in the implementation of the Vidyalaya’s Institutional Planning framed for the session 2019-2020.
